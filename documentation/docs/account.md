# Account and Settings

You can log into Shotly by either using your email and password or via Google.

!!! Info
    Shotly will never send spammy marketing emails or similar. The main use for your email is to provide a unique login as well as an option to reset your password. The only other time you will get an email from Shotly is if I personally reach out to you or if opt-in email Notifications become a feature.

You can manage your account at any time by clicking on `Account` in the lower left of the sidebar.

## Your Account

The first field in the Dialog informs you about your email address, the email cannot be changed and there is currently no way of transferring your data to another email.

Your "Display name" is a publicly visible name, I recommend simply making it your first name. The display name is used to identify when collaborating on a shotlist. You cannot use it to log into Shotly.

The third row allows you to manage or upgrade your subscription - you will be sent to the Stripe hosted page.

At the bottom of the dialog you can find the options to Sign out as well as delete your account. You can also request a feature or report a bug by opening a issue on GitHub.

## Settings

In this dialog you can also change your user settings:

- **Appearance**: Set to the sun icon for light mode, the moon for dark mode or the computer to use the system theme (default)
- **Remember export settings between reloads**: If turned on (default) your [export settings configuration](./shotlist/export.md#configuration) will be saved even after closing the page
- **Display Scene numbers next to Shot letters**: Will turn the shot letters from A, B, C (default) to 1A, 1B, 1C given that scene 1 is selected
- **Display shot numbers after Z as**: If more than 26 shots are created the shot letters will either double up and eventually triple up *or* mimic numbers and continue with AA, AB, AC and eventually turn to BA, BB, BC
- **Shotlist Scale**: A scaling factor that can be applied to the shotlist - double click to reset
