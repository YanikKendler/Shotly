# Archive

Shotlists can be archived by the shotlist owner via the `Shotlist Options` > `General` dialog.

!!! Info
    Archival is non-permanent, you can "unarchive" a shotlist at any time.

Archived shotlists will be in **read-only** mode, this means that no changes can be made to any part of the shotlist itself. It is still possible to `delete` and `unarchive` the shotlist as well as add new [collaborators](./collaboration.md).

Furthermore: archived shotlists will **no longer be displayed** on your [dashboard](https://shotly.at/dashboard) but instead in the [archive](https://shotly.at/dashboard/archive). You can access the archive from the dashboard by clicking on `Archive` in the bottom left of the sidebar or at the center of the screen just below the templates.

!!! Info
    A shotlists archival status will apply to all collaborators: it will be read-only and hidden in the [archive](https://shotly.at/dashboard/archive) for all collaborators and the owner.

!!! Info
    Archived shotlists still count towards your total shotlists, this means that if your account is in the "Basic" tier you will not be able to create a second shotlist. To create more than one shotlist you will need to upgrade to [Shotly Pro](https://shotly.at/pro).
